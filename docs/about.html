About Us

Longrun website is based on the hypothesis, that there is a large enough market segment that want to invest in all world stock index, but doesn't know how to. Our first concierge MVP is to help people open up Interactive Brokers accounts and guide them through how to buy. If there is significant traction then we will build a one-click all world stock index investing app.
