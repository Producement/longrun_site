Business account opening directions for Interactive Brokers

Why Interactive Brokers? Check out our reasoning here.

Starting the Application

  • Open new application page
  • Fill out the application and wait for the confirmation email
  • Click on the "Verify Account" button in the email
  • Log in with the credentials you just created

Account Type

  • Select "Institutional Accounts" and choose "Small Business"
  • Organization type should be "Limited Liability Company"

Organization Information

  • If you don't have a LEI code already, the cheapest place to get one is Bloomberg
  • Tax Identification Number is usually the company registration number, check here to be sure
  • Make sure to choose the proper currency for the account

Authorized Person Information

  • Fill out the form with your information
  • Tax Identification Number is usually your ID code, check here to be sure

Additional Information

  • No is selected by default and you can probably keep it

Tax information

  • Select "Corporation"

Regulatory Information

  • Select "A private company that is none of the above"

Financial Information

  • Make sure the account type is "Margin"
  • NB! If you want to open a Margin account the company net worth and liquid net worth has to be over 100,000
  • NB! If you want to open a Margin account you need to have at least 2 years of experience of trading on margin (11-25 trades) and "Good Knowledge"
  • NB! If you want to open a Margin account you need to have at least 2 years of experience with stocks (11-25 trades) and "Good Knowledge"

Directors

  • Make sure the entire board of directors are included here

Ownership

  • Add all owners of the company who have more than 25% stake in the company

Beneficial Owners

  • Sign the agreement by typing your name

W-8BEN-E

  • W-8BEN-E is a form needed by the US tax authority
  • Under "Chapter 3 status" select "Corporation"
  • Under "Chapter 4 status" select "Active NFFE"
  • Sign the agreement by typing your name

Tax Residency

  • Sign the agreement by typing your name

Identification Data

  • Click "Continue"

Affiliate and Public Symbol Designation

  • Click "Continue"

Review and Sign

  • Sign the agreement by typing your name
  • Click "Continue"
  • If you see this screen you can close the window and come back later
